    job summary:

    We are looking for an experienced Hyperion Planning and Essbase developer with solid technical skills and a firm understanding of finance, budgeting and forecasting principles. The Oracle Hyperion Planning Developer (FP &A) function will support, maintain, and enhance our current Planning environment, while working with the business to create new environments for our ever-growing customer demand. You will work cross functionally with the region FP &A teams, global FP &A teams, Consulting Operations, CS &S Operations, and IT to successfully deploy solutions to improve their processes. Additionally, this role will support the day-to-day maintenance and configuration of the Oracle Hyperion Planning Suite of products.
